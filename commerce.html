Commerce Courses in PUC

The Commerce stream in PUC offers a wide range of subject combinations that prepare students for careers in finance, business, and economics.

Popular Courses

  • CEBA (Commerce, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy)

    • Ideal for careers in accounting, finance, and business management.

  • SEBA (Statistics, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy)

    • Best suited for data analysis, finance, and economics enthusiasts.

  • HEBA (History, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy)

    • Perfect for those interested in social sciences and business.

  • MSBA (Mathematics, Statistics, Business Studies, Accountancy)

    • Focuses on mathematics and statistics, leading to careers in data analysis and actuarial science.
