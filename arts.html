Arts Courses in PUC

The Arts stream offers diverse subjects that explore human culture, history, and social sciences.

Popular Courses

  • HEPS (History, Economics, Political Science, Sociology)

    • Best for students interested in social sciences, public administration, and civil services.

  • HESP (History, Economics, Sociology, Psychology)

    • Focuses on psychology and sociology, suitable for careers in social work and counseling.

  • HEPL (History, Economics, Political Science, Literature)

    • Combines social sciences with literature, ideal for journalism and teaching.

  • JESP (Journalism, Economics, Sociology, Psychology)

    • Perfect for aspiring journalists, media professionals, and public relations experts.
