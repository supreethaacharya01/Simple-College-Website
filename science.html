Science Stream

The Science stream offers a courses

PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology)

Description: This course is ideal for students interested in both mathematics and biological sciences. It provides a strong foundation for careers in medicine, research, biotechnology, and more.

Potential Careers:

  • Doctor
  • Biotechnologist
  • Pharmacist
  • Research Scientist

PCMC (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science)

Description: This course is tailored for students interested in technology and computer applications. It opens up opportunities in engineering, IT, software development, and more.

Potential Careers:

  • Software Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • Computer Engineer
  • IT Consultant

PCMS (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Statistics)

Description: This course is focused on students who are interested in mathematics and analytics. It provides a strong base for careers in data science, finance, actuarial science, and more.

Potential Careers:

  • Statistician
  • Data Analyst
  • Actuary
  • Financial Analyst
